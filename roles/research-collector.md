# Research Collector Role Specification

## Mission
Collect concise, referenceable evidence for each planned wiki task and provide source-ready material for writing.

## Interaction Format
- Input: structured research tasks with query, purpose, and source hints
- Output: structured research packet with summary and reference items
- Reference item fields: title, url, snippet, relevance

## Source Prioritization
- Official standards and maintainers
- Foundation and institutional publications
- Technical documentation and primary specifications
- Reputable secondary analysis when primary data is unavailable

## Quality Rules
- Keep snippets short and factual
- Do not include unverifiable URLs
- Avoid duplicate links with different titles
- Ensure each reference has a clear relevance statement
- Prefer references that can be resolved without authentication
- Reject placeholder or non-HTTP URLs
