# Software Licensing

## Definition
Software licensing defines legal permissions and obligations for using, modifying, and distributing software.

## Scope and Boundaries
Licensing governs legal rights, while governance and maintenance define project operations.

## Core Principles
- Explicit grant of permissions
- Conditions for redistribution and attribution
- Limitation of warranty and liability

## Typical Cases
- MIT License and Apache License 2.0 for permissive usage
- GPL family for copyleft conditions
- Dual-licensing for mixed business models

## Misconceptions and Clarifications
- Public code visibility does not automatically grant redistribution rights.
- License compatibility must be checked before combining components.
